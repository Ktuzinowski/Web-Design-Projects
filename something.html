Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela in his later years, spreading peace

This tribute page was to honor the highly revered Nelson Mandela. Both a voice and symbol for change, the ending of discrimination, hatred, and animosity between two groups. Much can be learned on how to be tolerant of others, as Mandela sought to heal wounds instead of opening up new ones after the originally white dominated South African government and social structure was dismantled. He promoted Rugby amongst the black communities, spreading both pride and joy of a sport once only played by almost exclusively whites. Ending tensions between two groups once at war, and finding a path to move forward and progress. South Africa is now one of the worlds most prosperous economies, being a key geographical area for trade throughout the world. One can only wonder how Mandela would have continued to end rascism and promote the equality of all races and people. His story will live on forever.
